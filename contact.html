Common Inquiries

Standard shipping within the US typically takes 3-5 business days from the date of dispatch. Orders placed before noon EST are generally processed the same business day. International shipping timelines vary by destination, typically ranging from 7-14 business days.

We offer a seamless 30-day return policy for all unworn, unaltered items with original tags attached. Once your return is received at our distribution center, refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. Simply utilize the pre-paid shipping label included in your package.

For immediate assistance regarding time-sensitive issues (such as canceling an order or updating a shipping address before dispatch), please utilize our direct line at +1 (555) 123-4567 during standard working hours. For all other inquiries, our email support prioritizes responding within a single business day.
